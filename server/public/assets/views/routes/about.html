PRO INTEGRATION

THE SOLUTION IS PROVEN.

PRO Integration begins with pre-integration. Stakeholders and trades are coordinated. After equipment is fabricated, integrated and tested, it’s wired into racks, programmed and retested, and only then delivered for installation. Onsite integration and system launch includes placement of structured power, cable and hardware, along with mounting of displays and audio reinforcement. Audio, video and control are programmed and tested. And before the Acceptance of Substantial Completion is signed, we verify that the solution meets your needs.

AVI PRO INTEGRATION DELIVERS:

  • Execution of Scope of Work
  • Outlined time frames
  • Dedicated project manager
  • Industry-trained and certified integration team
  • Control system programming
  • System commissioning and certification
  • System operational training
  • 90-day warranty
